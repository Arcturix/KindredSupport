Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Kindred ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create and manage your family tree and relationships
  • Add personal details about family members (names, dates of birth, contact information)
  • Upload photos and add notes about family members
  • Use our contact import feature
  • Subscribe to our Pro features

Device Information

We may collect information about your device, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage statistics and crash reports
  • Unique device identifiers

Cloud Data

When you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have direct access to this data, and it is subject to Apple's iCloud privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our family tree services
  • Enable data synchronization across your devices
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Process Pro subscription payments
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important updates about our service

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data encryption both in transit and at rest
  • Secure data storage using industry-standard practices
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls to limit who can view your data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

iCloud Integration

When you use iCloud sync:

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Data is encrypted by Apple's iCloud security
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of certain data processing
  • Export your data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
