Getting Started

New to Kindred? Here's how to get started:

  • Download and install the app from the App Store
  • Create your first family member by tapping the "+" button in the People tab
  • Add relationships by connecting family members
  • Add events, notes, and photos to bring your family tree to life
  • Upgrade to PRO for iCloud sync and backup features

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new family member?

Go to the People tab and tap the "+" button, then fill in the person's details. You can add photos, notes, contact information, and relationship roles.

How does iCloud sync work? (PRO Feature)

iCloud sync keeps your family tree data synchronized across all your Apple devices. This is a PRO feature that requires a one-time $4.99 purchase. Your data is encrypted and stored securely in your personal iCloud account.

Can I import contacts from my phone?

Yes! Go to Settings > Import Contacts to add people from your phone's contact list to your family tree.

What are PRO features?

PRO features include iCloud sync, CloudKit backup, and cross-device synchronization. You can upgrade in Settings > Pro Upgrade for a one-time $4.99 purchase.

How do I backup my data?

PRO users can use the CloudKit backup feature in Settings > iCloud Backup. This creates encrypted backups of your data in your personal iCloud account.

How do I add events and reminders?

In the People tab, tap on any person to view their details, then scroll down to the Events section to add birthdays, anniversaries, and other important dates.

Troubleshooting

App won't open

  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space

iCloud sync issues (PRO users)

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify iCloud is enabled in your device settings
  • Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices
  • Try disabling and re-enabling iCloud sync in Settings
  • Make sure you have an active PRO subscription

Data not saving

  • Check your device storage space
  • Ensure the app has necessary permissions
  • Try restarting the app
