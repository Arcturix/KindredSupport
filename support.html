Getting Started

New to Kindred? Here's how to get started:

  • Download and install the app from the App Store
  • Create your first family member by tapping the "+" button
  • Add relationships by connecting family members
  • Enable iCloud sync to keep your data safe across devices

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new family member?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, then fill in the person's details. You can add photos, notes, and contact information.

How does iCloud sync work?

iCloud sync keeps your family tree data synchronized across all your Apple devices. Enable it in Settings > iCloud Sync. Your data is encrypted and stored securely in your personal iCloud account.

Can I import contacts from my phone?

Yes! Go to Settings > Import Contacts to add people from your phone's contact list to your family tree.

What are Pro features?

Pro features include unlimited family members, advanced relationship types, custom tags, and priority support. You can upgrade in Settings > Pro Upgrade.

How do I backup my data?

With iCloud sync enabled, your data is automatically backed up. You can also use the CloudKit backup feature in Settings for additional security.

Can I export my family tree?

Yes, you can export your family tree data in various formats. Go to Settings > Export Data to choose your preferred format.

Troubleshooting

App won't open

  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space

iCloud sync issues

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify iCloud is enabled in your device settings
  • Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices
  • Try disabling and re-enabling iCloud sync in the app

Data not saving

  • Check your device storage space
  • Ensure the app has necessary permissions
  • Try restarting the app
